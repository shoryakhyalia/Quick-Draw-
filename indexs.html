Why My Mom Is The Best Mom In The World

I love her not because she is my mother and we should respect our elders. I respect

her because she has taken care of me when I was not able to speak. At that time, she

has taken care of all my needs when I wasn’t able to speak. Additionally, she

taught me how to walk, speak, and take care of myself. Similarly, every bigger step

that I have taken in my life is all because of my mother. Because, if she hasn’t taught

me how to take small steps then I won’t be able to take these bigger step.

She is an essence of truthfulness, love, and sincerity. Another reason is that she

showers her family with her blessing and live. Furthermore, she gives us everything

but never demand anything in return. The way she cares for everyone in the family

inspires me to the same in my future. Also, her love is not just for the family

she treats every stranger and animals the same way she did to me. Due to, this

she is very kind and sensible towards the environment and animals.
